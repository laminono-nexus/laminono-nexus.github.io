Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026  ·  Laminono Nexus

Laminono Nexus uses a very small number of cookies — only what is necessary to keep the platform running and to understand how readers use it. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (phone, tablet, or computer) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with them.

Cookies cannot harm your device and do not contain viruses or malware. They are simply small data files used to improve your experience.

2. How Laminono Nexus Uses Cookies

We use cookies in two ways on this platform:

  • Essential functionality: To remember your reading preferences and locally stored data like ratings and comments within the same session.
  • Analytics: To understand how many people visit, which stories are popular, and how readers navigate the site — all anonymously through Google Analytics.

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Laminono Nexus uses your browser's local storage to save your ratings and comments. This data never leaves your device — it is stored only in your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache and local storage in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics is the only third-party service we use that sets cookies. Google may use this data according to their own privacy policy. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use cookies from advertising networks, social media trackers, or any other third-party services.

6. How to Control Cookies

You have full control over cookies on your device. You can:

  • Block all cookies: Go to your browser settings and disable cookies. Note that this may affect how some parts of the site function.
  • Delete existing cookies: Clear your browser's cookies and cache at any time through your browser settings.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

On mobile browsers, you can manage cookies through your phone's browser settings (usually under Privacy or Advanced settings).

7. Do We Use Advertising Cookies?

No. Laminono Nexus does not use any advertising cookies, remarketing cookies, or tracking pixels from advertising networks. We do not display ads on this platform, and we do not allow advertisers to track you through our site.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new features that require additional cookies, we will update this Cookie Policy and revise the "Last updated" date. We will always be transparent about what cookies we use and why.
