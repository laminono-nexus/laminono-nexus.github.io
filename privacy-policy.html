Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026  ·  Laminono Nexus

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains clearly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. Laminono Nexus is a storytelling platform — we do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Laminono Nexus is a premium storytelling platform founded and operated by Olamide Emmanuel Willoughby, also known as Laminono. The platform is accessible at laminono-nexus.github.io and is dedicated to sharing original stories across genres including Drama, Sci-Fi, Dystopian, Romance, and more.

For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: laminonosfilmview@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience on the platform:

  • Usage data: Pages you visit, stories you read, time spent on pages. This is collected anonymously through Google Analytics.
  • Comments: If you leave a comment on a story, the text you submit is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and is not transmitted to any external server.
  • Ratings: Star ratings you give to stories are stored locally in your browser and are not shared.
  • Coin support actions: When you tap a coin support button, this action is recorded locally. No payment or financial data is collected at this time.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Understand which stories are most popular and improve the platform
  • Monitor website performance and fix technical issues
  • Improve the reading experience for all visitors

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our platform. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as your approximate location, device type, browser type, and which pages you visit. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies

Laminono Nexus uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics. These are small files stored in your browser that help us track website visits anonymously. We do not use cookies to track your personal identity or behaviour outside our website.

Please see our Cookie Policy for full details.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external platforms including Instagram, Twitter/X, and YouTube. When you click these links, you leave Laminono Nexus and are subject to the privacy policies of those platforms. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

Laminono Nexus is intended for readers aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Security

Since we collect minimal personal data, your risk exposure is very low. Comments and ratings are stored only in your own browser and are never transmitted to our servers. We use GitHub Pages for hosting, which provides secure HTTPS connections for all visitors.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at laminonosfilmview@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the platform grows. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Questions about your privacy?
